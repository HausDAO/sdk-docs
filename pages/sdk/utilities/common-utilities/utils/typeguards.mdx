# [`typeguards.ts`](https://github.com/HausDAO/daohaus-monorepo/blob/develop/libs/common-utilities/src/utils/typeguards.ts)

Series of user-defined typeguards that help verify the shape of data.

- `isArray`
- `isNumber`
- `isString`
- `isBoolean`
- `isNumberish`
- `isEthAddress`
- `isArgType`
- `isNumberString`
- `isLengthOf`

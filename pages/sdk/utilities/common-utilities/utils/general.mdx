# [`general.ts`](https://github.com/HausDAO/daohaus-monorepo/blob/develop/libs/common-utilities/src/utils/general.ts)

Calculate the voting power percentage for a member of a DAO. Return the current time in seconds. Calculate the share of a member's token balance compared to the total number of shares in a DAO. Calculate the USD value share of a member in a DAO, based on the total USD value of the DAO, the total shares in the DAO, and the number of shares the member has.

## Functions

- `votingPowerPercentage`
- `nowInSeconds`
- `memberTokenBalanceShare`
- `memberUsdValueShare`
